Electric Trike

Construction

30 July 2022

Wheelchairs have been a symbol of empowerment and independence to the differently-abled through the years. These machines have given people mobility without the need to depend on anyone else. There have been several developments and changes in these machines since they were first used. Developments like motorization and automation have made them more dependable. But for the less fortunate parts of the society, they would consume large parts of their income. The weaker sections of our society seldom have the freedom to choose a wheelchair of their needs. The first reason is that most of the wheelchairs are designed for urban homes and offices. Rarely wheelchairs are seen in rural areas and outdoors where the roads are rough and dusty. The second reason is that they cannot afford wheelchairs and their maintenance. There is need of a Tricycle that is made in accordance to both the aspects of the situation.

The proposed design is a hybrid of hand powered and electric tricycle. Large front wheels make moving in rough terrains easy. The battery provides backup of several hours. The electric motor powers the Tricycle and reduces the efforts of the driver. The seat is comfortable and adjustable according to the userâ€™s height. The frame is sturdy and durable. The weight is distributed equally to the wheels. The whole system is designed to require minimum maintenance. The front wheel is hand powered. The handle connected to a chain is rotated by the user which powers the front wheel. The rear wheels can be powered by an electric motor placed under the seat. The power is transferred from the motor to the wheels by a small speed reduction gearbox connecting the axle. The motor can be switched on or off depending on the need of the user. The simultaneous power supply from both motor and the user will reduce effort to be applied by the user. The user can also choose not to hand pedal the Tricycle for certain intervals of time.

  • Main frame: 1 inch diameter 2mm thickness AISI 4130 steel tubes for the main frame and 0.5 inch diameter 2 mm thickness AISI 4130 steel tubes used for auxiliary frame parts
  • Front wheel: 20 cm diameter front wheel connected to handle manually driven
  • Motor and batteries:350 watt motor with a motor controller powered by rechargeable batteries
  • Battery provide power only when needed, like on inclined surface. Otherwise it can be driven using manpower.
  • Person of weight upto 100kg (in prototype) can use it.
